About the project

Culture, creativity and digital skills

Our Culture invites young people to discover, document and share the cultural heritage that shapes their communities — using today's digital tools as a bridge between generations.

  • Digital literacy

    Helping young people navigate online spaces confidently, safely and creatively.

  • Cultural storytelling

    Turning traditions, places and people into stories worth hearing — through audio, video and text.

  • Creative exchange

    Connecting youth across borders to share culture, ideas and new ways of learning together.

13–14 November 2025 • Paris, France

OURCULTURE Kick-off in Paris

On 13–14 November 2025, our European consortium came together in Paris to launch OURCULTURE — a project co-funded by the European Union (2025-1-FR02-KA210-YOU-000360078) empowering youth with digital skills, media literacy and creative storytelling to celebrate and preserve cultural heritage.

During this first transnational meeting, partners from France, Turkey and Hungary gathered to align on the project's shared vision, define the management and communication framework, and set the creative direction for the months ahead.

  • ✅ Shared management and communication tools adopted
  • ✅ Risk Management and Ethics plans confirmed
  • ✅ Roadmap milestones set for the Action Guide

The Our Culture project empowers young people across Europe with digital literacy, media skills and creative storytelling to celebrate and preserve cultural heritage. Stay tuned for more updates as we move forward together!

Project Partners

Three countries, one shared vision

Our Culture is built by a small consortium of organisations who share a belief in the power of youth, culture and collaboration.

France

eutopique

A French non-profit fostering positive educational transitions through design thinking, ethnographic research and collective intelligence. eutopique empowers children and young people aged 7–30 around cultural heritage, sustainability and digital transformation.

Coordinator Visit website

Turkey

Akdeniz Panorama Derneği

A Turkish non-profit founded in 2021 that promotes education, media literacy, gender equality, environmental sustainability and social entrepreneurship. Akdeniz Panorama works with children, young people and adults, with a special focus on those from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Partner Visit website

Hungary

Fiatalok Egységben Egyesület

A Hungarian youth association promoting intercultural understanding, youth empowerment and digital transformation. Youth Together Association fosters civic participation, creative expression and youth-led international cooperation.

Partner Visit Facebook page
